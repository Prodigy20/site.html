Barrister Yusuf Olalere emerges as Nigeria's Overall Best Graduating Law Student of 2020

Barrister Yusuf Asamu Olalere, has emerged as Nigeria's Overall Best Graduating Law Student of 2020. He was called to bar yesterday alongside his colleagues.

Yusuf has taken to his Twitter page to celebrate his new success as Nigeria's Overall Best Graduating Law Student of 2020. Yesterday, over 1,700 Law students were called to bar in Abuja.

Nollywood veteran actor, Kanayo O. Kanayo was also part of the Law student that was called to bar yesterday. He's a graduate of Uni Abuja. (Read More Here)

Yusuf graduated with a double first class; one first class from the university and the other from Law school. He also won 12 awards as the Overall Best Graduating Law Student of 2020. Congratulations Yusuf Asamu Olalere!

"Eternally grateful to God for the grace. I was not only called to the largest Bar in Africa today, I also emerged as the Overall Best Graduating Student of the Nigerian Law Student this year," he tweeted.
